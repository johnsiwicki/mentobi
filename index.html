What is Mentobi?

At Mentobi, we believe writing is a deeply personal thing. Mentobi is an online collaboration network for writers of all skill levels. Our community allows you to work on your craft, ask questions and get help from creators. If you've been sitting on a blog draft, need help with an essay, or have an idea for an ebook, Mentobi is the place to brainstorm and explore your idea.
